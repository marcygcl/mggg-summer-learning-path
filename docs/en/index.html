This is my learning journal. I'm Marcy Castro Livano, a data scientist with a background in geospatial and census work, spending the summer of 2026 at the MGGG Data and Democracy Lab.

My project is to replicate MGGG's electoral-analysis methodology for Kansas City — modelling what representation would look like under ranked-choice and single-transferable-vote systems instead of plurality. I don't have all the pieces yet, so I'm documenting the learning itself: what I explored each week, the moments something clicked, the code I came to understand, and the questions I'm carrying into the next week.

I keep it here for myself — to think clearly and have something to return to — and for the next intern who starts where I'm starting now.
