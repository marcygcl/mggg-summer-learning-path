# Learning Materials & Resources

## Videos Created

### NotebookLM: Census Fundamentals (Spanish)
- **Duration:** 22 minutes
- **Generated from:** VAP-CVAP.pdf + Massachusetts RCV Report
- **Topics Covered:**
  - What is VAP vs CVAP
  - How they're calculated from P3/P4 Census tables
  - Why both are needed
  - Demographic categories (BVAP, HVAP, etc.)
- **Format:** Audio discussion with visual explainer style

### NotebookLM: Electoral Systems (English)
- **Duration:** 25 minutes
- **Generated from:** Lowell Report + Massachusetts RCV Report + VoteKit documentation
- **Topics Covered:**
  - Plurality voting mechanics
  - Instant Runoff Voting (IRV) step-by-step
  - Single Transferable Vote (STV) for multi-winner elections
  - Comparison of representation outcomes
  - Why electoral systems matter
- **Format:** Video with whiteboard explanations

## Key Documents

- **VAP-CVAP.pdf** - MGGG's official guide to Census demographic construction
- **Lowell Report** - Ranked Choice Voting analysis for Lowell, MA city elections
- **Massachusetts RCV Report** - State-level RCV impact analysis
